More About Ezra

Composer

Described as "energetic and good-humored," Ezra's music has been performed at New York's Carnegie Hall and throughout the world. Inquire about commissioning a new work!

Conductor

As choral, orchestral, and opera conductor, Ezra has received praise and recognition for his "nuanced" and "outstanding" performances of the operettas of Gilbert and Sullivan.

Pianist

Ezra has excelled in piano competitions and performances since the age of 7, with extensive experience as a collaborative pianist. Available for recitals, parties, and other events.
